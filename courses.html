Cheese, Yoghurt and Butter Making

This 3 day course guides trainees as to how to produce
both hard and soft cheese, yoghurt and butter and provides
the ingredients for the participants to make their own.

Course Content:

  • Day 1:
  • Introduction to milk and milk compostion
  • Manufacture of Gouda style cheese
  • Manufacture of Yoghurt
  • Microbiology and starter bacteria
  • Manufacture of Buttermilk and Soft fresh Cheese
  • Day 2:
  • Evaluation of products from Day 1
  • Students hands on manufacture of Cheese and Yoghurt
  • Salting/Brining of Cheese
  • Other Cheese varieties
  • Day 3:
  • Evaluation of products from Day 2
  • Manufacture of Butter
  • Manufacture of downstream products
  • Design of Premises and Regulations
  • Overview including Q & A Session

Outcomes:

Students will have the theoretical and practical
knowledge to make both hard and soft cheese,
yoghurt and butter products and be advised of the plant
equipment required to produce.
