Pest

The European corn borer is primarily a pest of corn, but pepper and eggplants are also relatively common hosts. In the spring adults lay their eggs on plants and the emerging larvae bore into the stalks.

Pest

The tarnished plant bug is a sukling insect that feeds on flowers and buds of eggplants, pepper and tomato causing flower drop, which greatly reduces yield in some years.

Solution

  • Floating row covers.
  • Good weed control
  • Short crop rotation
